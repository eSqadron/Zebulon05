Step.msg:
type - type of step
0 - full step (lift leg and move it forward; put leg on the ground; move it to the back; lift it up; move to the neutral position and put on the ground)
1 - forward step (lift leg and move it forward; put leg on the ground)
2 - push off (move leg to the back)
3 - reset leg (lift leg up and return to neutral position; put it on the ground)
